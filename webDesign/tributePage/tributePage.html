Marcus Aurelius

The Philosopher King

His Timeline

  • 121 CE - Born
  • 132 CE - Introduced to philosophy by his teacher Diognetus
  • 138 CE - Adopted by Emperor Antoninus Pius as successor
  • 145 CE - Marries Faustina, daughter of Antoninus Pius
  • 161 CE - Becomes Emperor of the Roman Empire with Lucius Verus
  • 161 - 166CE - Campaigns against the Parthians
  • 169 CE - Death of Lucius Verus, Marcus Aurelius becomes sole ruler
  • 170 - 180 CE - Campaigns against the Germanic tribes, writes his "Meditations"
  • 177 - 180 CE - Commodus become his co-emperor and heir
  • 180 CE - Death

Famous quotes

  • “You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this, and you will find strength.”
  • “Dwell on the beauty of life. Watch the stars, and see yourself running with them.”
  • “The happiness of your life depends upon the quality of your thoughts.”
  • “Everything we hear is an opinion, not a fact. Everything we see is a perspective, not the truth.”
  • “When you arise in the morning think of what a privilege it is to be alive, to think, to enjoy, to love ...”
